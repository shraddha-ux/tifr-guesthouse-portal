About TIFR Guest House

The TIFR Guesthouse building in the Colaba Campus is named after the great Indian mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan.


It consists of three different categories of rooms. In addition, the facility provides fully furnished and serviced studios and two-bedroom apartments on campus for long-term guests.


Rooms can be booked online using TIFR Datanet by authorized members. For all others, booking requests should be sent to: guesthouse@tifr.res.in
