Catering Services

The Colaba campus of TIFR has several eateries which collectively provide services from 8:00 AM to 2:00 AM for staff, residents and guests. In addition, the guesthouse provides breakfast and dinner services in the in-house dining hall at Ramanujan House.

Ramanujan Cafeteria

Dining Hall Services

  • Breakfast: 8.00 AM – 10.00 AM (included in room rent)
  • Lunch is not provided in the Guesthouse Cafeteria
  • Dinner: 8.00 PM – 9.30 PM
  • Prior request required before 6.30 PM
  • Vegetarian Dinner: ₹100
  • Non-Vegetarian Dinner: ₹125
